How To Fix Spotify Podcast Not Playing Or Working On Phone Or Desktop 2022

Publish date: 2024-07-23

How to fix Spotify Podcast not playing or working on phone or desktop 2022;- Are you one of the many Spotify users who are experiencing the Spotify podcast not working on their desktop or phone? Many users have reported this issue recently. We offer solutions to the problem of Spotify Podcast not playing or freezing.

Fix Spotify Podcast not playing or working on phone or desktop

Spotify Podcast is the only affected, but all music playlists work as normal. Some users believe the problem is related to a connection issue when searching for the podcast.

How to fix Spotify Podcast not Playing 2022

How to fix Spotify Podcast not working on your phone or computer 2022.

Below is how to fix Spotify Podcast not Working 2022 on iPhone 12, iPad, Samsung Galaxy, Pixel, etc.

1. DNS/Check for blocked servers

Spotify users who use Adblockers might have issues with Spotify Podcast not working. These are DNS-based ones. This blocked all podcasts from working. You can whitelist the server and fix the problem.

2. Uninstall and re-install

Spotify podcast is not working? Simply uninstall the app from the device and restart the phone or computer. Once the phone is turned on, you can reinstall Spotify and log in to your account. Next, check that podcasts work as normal.

3. Look out for the latest update

You can also try updating the app to fix the problem. Go to the playstore for Android and the appstore on iPhone. Search for the app and check for any updates. If you see a new version, update it to check if Spotify Podcast works.

3. Give it a while

Spotify Podcast’s feature may not be working properly or is playing due to server problems or software bugs. These issues are quickly fixed by the support team when they occur. Please wait for a while and then check again to see if the feature is working correctly.

4. Spotify Support

If the above methods are not sufficient and you have further questions, you can ask our global Community of expert supporters. https://support.spotify.com/
