Heres What Happened to the Iron Throne

Publish date: 2024-08-10

For eight seasons, we’ve been wondering who’s going to end up on Game of Thrones’ Iron Throne, assuming the seat itself would still be around. Sunday night’s series finale had a little surprise: It melted! After Jon Snow stabbed Daenerys Targaryen, Drogon — the last remaining dragon — threw a tantrum and melted the throne that led his mother to her demise. (A fitting end: “The breath of the greatest dragon forged the Iron Throne.
