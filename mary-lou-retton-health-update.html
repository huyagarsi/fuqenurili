Mary Lou Retton Health Update: What Did She Say About Her Condition?

Publish date: 2024-06-11

Mary Lou Retton got sick in early October and has been in the hospital. She’s a famous Olympic gymnast, and her family hasn’t shared a lot about her health, but they did say she’s dealing with a rare and serious kind of pneumonia.

Mary Lou doesn’t have health insurance, which means her medical bills could be really high. To help pay for her care, her daughters are collecting donations. Here’s what we know about Mary Lou Retton’s health so far.

Mary Lou Retton Health Update

Olympic gymnastics star Mary Lou Retton said thanks on Monday to everyone who helped her during a tough time with pneumonia.

Retton said in an Instagram post, “I am overwhelmed with the love and support from the world as I fight. I am forever grateful to you all! I’m with family continuing to slowly recover and staying very positive as I know this recovery is a long and slow process.”

Last week, the family shared that Retton is out of the hospital. She had a rare pneumonia that made it hard for her to breathe, and she couldn’t breathe on her own. It was a tough time but now she’s getting better at home after spending weeks in the hospital.

According to her daughter, McKenna Kelley, it might take a while for her to recover and be back to 100% healthfully.

On October 18, her daughters shared that she faced a setback in getting better, even though she had started to improve before. A few days ago, they shared on Instagram that Retton’s “breathing is becoming stronger.”
