Tre Tre Thornton is the son of Adolph Robert Thornton Jr., the late celebrated rapper better known by his stage name, Young Dolph, and Mia Jaye. He was born on June 20, 2014, in Memphis, Tennessee, United States. The 9-year-old Tre Tre is the only son of Young Dolph Thornton but he has a younger sister named Aria Ella Thornton, who was born in February 2017.

Tre Tre was introduced to the public spotlight through his father’s fame. Unfortunately, his father, Young Dolph, was tragically killed in November 2021 when Tre Tre was just seven years old. Tre Tre Thornton is sharing his childhood with a younger sister and together, the kids have been growing up under the watch of their mother after their father’s death.

How Old Is Tre Tre Thornton?

Tre Tre Thornton is currently 9 years old as he was born on the 20th of June 2014. The young American was born in Memphis, Tennessee, where he is spending his formative years.

The name “Tre Tre” is French and means the number three – an acknowledgment that the boy has become the third family member among the Thorntons. His nationality is obviously American but Tre Tre is of mixed ethnicity.

As earlier mentioned, he is the son of the late American hip hop rapper, Young Dolph, who goes by the official name Adolph Robert Thornton Jr. Dolph lost his life after he was shot 22 times in front of a Memphis bakery on November, 17th, 2021.

His mother, on the other hand, has been identified as Mia Jaye – an entrepreneur with the retail fashion brand known as Shop MoMeo. Mia is an alum of the University of Memphis where she bagged a B.Sc. in Real Estate Finance in 2012.

She has been leveraging her brand to embrace campaigns for several social causes, including #BlackMenDeserveToGrowOld and #BlackLivesMatter. The former was inspired by Dolph and her brother who lost their lives in similar circumstances.

Talking about his academics, the 9-year-old is currently in the process of acquiring his early education but the school he is attending has never been mentioned in the public space. This may be the family’s way of being security conscious given that the boy’s father was recently killed by unknown gunmen.

Tre Tre is the first fruit of Young Dolph and Mia Jaye’s relationship.
