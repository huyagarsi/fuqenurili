Scott Rasmussen Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Publish date: 2024-07-28
Scott W. Rasmussen (born March 30, 1956) is a political commentator and digital media entrepreneur in the United States. Rasmussen is a public opinion pollster, lecturer, and New York Times bestselling book. Rasmussen’s political opinions are published weekly in a nationally syndicated newspaper column. Rasmussen Reports is his company, and he is the creator and former president. Rasmussen departed Rasmussen Reports in July 2013 to start Rasmussen Media Group, LLC, a digital media company whose goal is to “refine, expand, and empower the voice of mainstream America.” Rasmussen was a co-founder of the sports network ESPN earlier in his career.
