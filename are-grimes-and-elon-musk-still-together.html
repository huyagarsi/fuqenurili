Are Grimes and Elon Musk still together?

Publish date: 2024-07-22

The relationship of celebrity couple Grimes and Elon Musk is admired by many people even though they are stable.

They are most at times not together despite having two kids.

To add up rumors of their separation, Elon Musk said in 2021 that they were semi- separated.

In a recent interview Grimes also opened up about their relationship.

“We live in separate houses. We’re best friends. We see each other all the time… We just have our own thing going on, and I don’t expect other people to understand it.”

She also went on to add that  “Me and E have broken up *again* since the writing of this article haha, but he’s my best friend and the love of my life, and my life and art are forever dedicated to The Mission now.”

Are Grimes and Elon Musk still together?

Grimes and Elon Musk are not together anymore, Grimes has said.

Who is Grimes and Elon Musk’s secret child?
