Renee Young net worth and salary: Renee Young is a Canadian sports broadcaster who has a net worth of $2 million. Renee Young was born in Toronto, Ontario, Canada in September 1985. She is best known for being an on-air personality with the WWE. Young became a color commentator for the television series Raw in 2018 when she became the first woman to call an entire episode. Young started out in improvisational comedy and moved to Los Angeles, California at 19 years old.

She worked for The Score Television Network. Renee Young signed with the WWE in 2012 and debuted in 2013 on SmackDown as an interviewer. She made her commentary debut on NXT in 2013.
