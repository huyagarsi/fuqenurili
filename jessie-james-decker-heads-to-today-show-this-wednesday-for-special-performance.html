Jessie James Decker Heads to TODAY Show this Wednesday for Special Performance Celeb Secrets Cou

Publish date: 2024-06-03

Jessie James Decker is heading to the Big Apple this week!

The country singer/songwriter and style entrepreneur will make an appearance on NBC’s TODAY with Kathie Lee and Hoda this Wednesday, August 31 to perform her new single, “Lights Down Low.”

Written by Decker and Alyssa Bonagura, “Lights Down Low” is inspired by the quiet moments Decker and husband Eric still manage to find as the couple raise two children and juggle busy careers.

Jessie James Decker is signed to Epic Records (Sony) and the new single, “Lights Down Low” is impacting country radio in September, helmed by Thirty Tigers and New Revolution.

Tune in August 31 at 10am ET/PT on NBC to catch the performance!
