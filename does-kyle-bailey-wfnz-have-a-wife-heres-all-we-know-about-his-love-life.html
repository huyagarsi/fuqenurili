Does Kyle Bailey WFNZ Have A Wife? Married Life, Where Is He Now?

Yes, Kyle Bailey of WFNZ has been married to a wife. He and his wife Camille Bailey are parents to a beautiful baby daughter.

Bailey met his wife for the first time eight years ago in Blacksburg. They dated for some years before getting engaged in October 2017. The two tied their knots in October 2018.

The couple became parents to a baby girl, Skye in March 2021. On Christmas 2021, Kyle announced that Camille is pregnant with their second child.

Currently, the North Carolina-based radio personality is currently the host of the popular radio talk show “The Clubhouse with Kyle Bailey”, which he hosts with Evan Ludvign and a rotating host every weekday from 2 to 6 pm.

Also, Kyle runs a podcast along with former American football player Roman Harper. The podcast titled Baily & Harper show is a fun show where the two talk about sports-related activities and news.
