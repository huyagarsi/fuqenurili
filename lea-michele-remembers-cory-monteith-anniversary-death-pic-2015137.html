It’s hard to believe that it’s already been two years since Glee star Cory Monteith passed away. But Lea Michele refuses to let his memory die. 

On Monday, July 13, the Scream Queens actress, 28, took to Twitter to share a sweet silhouette image of Monteith driving a car. 

“Today we remember the laughter and joy you brought into our lives every day. We think of you always and love you so,” she wrote of her late costar and boyfriend.

Monteith was found dead in his Vancouver hotel room at age 31 back in 2013 after suffering an overdose from a combination of alcohol and heroin. 

Michele, who was dating the actor at the time of his death, has since moved on and lives with her new boyfriend, Matthew Paetz. 

“Matthew, thank you for bringing such happiness into my life,” the “If You Say So” singer wrote in May. “For making me smile again and bringing such a bright joy into my every day…Thank you for loving me and my family…And of course Sheila too…May this be the happiest Birthday ever!”
