Heres How to Tell Jason &amp; Brett Oppenheim Apart The Selling Sunset Twins Have a Few Dif

Publish date: 2024-08-31

One question that a lot of Selling Sunset viewers probably have is how to tell twins Jason and Brett Oppenheim apart, so let’s figure it out together!

The Oppenheim twins are the co-owners of real estate firm The Oppenheim Group and they’re identical, so it’s very hard to tell them apart from a quick glance.

There thankfully are a few differences that will help you figure it out if you’re looking closely though!

Click inside to read more…

  • 1. Smile!

    One of the major differences between the brothers is how they smile. Jason has a wider smile that shows both his upper and lower teeth while Brett has a thinner smile that just shows the upper teeth.

  • 2. Face Shape!

    If you look closely, fans think they notice that Brett has a more square face and a thicker jaw while Jason has a pointier chin and a more pronounced jawline.

  • 3. Height!

    Brett appears to be slightly taller than Jason when they are standing next to each other.

  • 4. Facial Hair!

    You might notice that Brett’s facial hair appears to be a bit darker in color than Jason’s.

  • 5. Voice!

    Brett has a lower and more coarse tone to his voice than Jason.
