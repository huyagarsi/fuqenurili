Paris Hilton, Hannah Ferguson Star in Carl's Jr. Ad: Exclusive Picture

Publish date: 2024-05-20

These ladies sure love “Texas in the springtime.” Nearly a decade after her original commercial, Paris Hilton returned for yet another steamy Carl’s Jr. ad, and this time she brought along a friend—Sports Illustrated model Hannah Ferguson. 

The bikini-clad gals munch on a Texas BBQ thickburger, but it’s 22-year-old Ferguson that gets soapy during the commercial. Hilton, 33, makes a quick cameo toward the end, quipping, “You missed a spot.”

The hotel heiress first stripped down for a Carl’s Jr. ad in 2005. The commercial was banned from TV at the time for its over-the-top sexual nature.
