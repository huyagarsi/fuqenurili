Alfred Molina weds Frozen director Jennifer Lee at ceremony officiated by Jonathan Groff

Publish date: 2024-07-20

Actor Alfred Molina revealed he tied the knot with Oscar-winning Frozen director Jennifer Lee via social media on Monday.

Lee, 49, posted a stunning gallery of photos from the sunlit ceremony with the sweet caption, ‘We did. We do.’ 

In one snap, Molina, 68, was seen overcome with emotion as he watched his bride come down the aisle. 

Overcome with emotion: As revealed on Instagram on Monday, actor Alfred Molina has tied the knot with Oscar-winning Frozen director Jennifer Lee

The couple was also seen embracing in two snaps, backed by greenery and beautiful rose bushes.

Jennifer wore a lovely sequined white dress, white Spider-Man 2 star Molina placed a white rose in the lapel of his dark suit.   

Also present at the ceremony as officiator was actor Jonathan Groff, who famously voiced Kristoff in the Frozen films.

In a subsequent post from Lee, she shared further details of how Groff, 36, was involved in her nuptials to Molina.

Next to a photo of Jonathan and Jennifer talking animatedly at the reception, she wrote in the caption: ‘This beautiful, generous soul is the reason Fred and I met. We owe him the world.’ 

The couple, who have been dating for over two years, look to have gotten married in their backyard over the weekend.

Alfred was previously married to English actress Jill Gascoine, with whom he shares a daughter, Rachel, 41.

Gascoine died after a battle with Alzheimer’s disease in April of 2020, at the age of 83. 

She and Molina had been married since 1986.

Jennifer, meanwhile, was previously married to Robert Joseph Monn, with whom she shares daughter Agatha. 

Agatha, 18, was the voice of 9-year-old Anna in the first Frozen film, from 2013. 

And Molina was the voice of Agnarr in 2019’s Frozen II, also co-directed by Lee.
