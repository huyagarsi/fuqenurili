How did the ButterCream gang get their name?

The kids who make up the movie’s “ButterCream Gang” gather to do good deeds around the fictional rural town of Elkridge. The title, it is explained, was coined years earlier when local boys were asked to help widows churn cream into butter.

Is the ButterCream Gang a Mormon movie?

THE BUTTERCREAM GANG is entertaining and wholesome, but because it is produced by the predominantly Mormon company Feature Films for Families, Christians may want to approach it with discernment.
