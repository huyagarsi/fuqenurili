Harry Shum Jr. Marries Shelby Rabara

Publish date: 2024-05-24

According to Us Weekly Glee star Harry Shum Jr. married his longtime girlfriend Shelby Rabara in Costa Rica this weekend. The couple got engaged two years ago and have been together since 2007. A source told Us Weekly that “they have been together for years and are so great together.” The newlyweds share a home together in Los Angeles and they also have a dog named Charlie.

Harry Shum Jr.’s former costars Amber Riley, Jenna Ushkowitz, and Kevin McHale were all in attendance at the wedding which was held in Shum’s home country Costa Rica. There are no official photos from the ceremony yet, however, Ushkowitz and McHale shared a couple of photos from the destination. One of them was captioned:

Thank you @qualityitalianny for the delicious meal and the small chicken parm child I conceived this evening. #weddingbaby

Harry Shum Jr. is best known for Glee where he played Mike Chang during all six seasons. He now stars as Magnus Band on Shadowhunters which airs on ABC Family. His wife Shelby Rabara also starred on Glee in its first season as Shoshandra who is a part of Vocal Adrenaline, the rivals of New Directions.

Congratulations to the happy couple! CelebMix wishes them all the best in the future.
