Gabe Guest House, Kasoa, Ghana

Publish date: 2024-09-11

Gabe Guest House is a budget friendly facility situated in a serene environment in Accra, Ghana. The guest house is 30 minutes away from West Hills Mall, 5 minutes away from the ocean, and free WiFi is available within the premises.

Each room has an en-suite bathroom, a wardrobe, a refrigerator, an air conditioner and a TV.

The guest house serves breakfast at a surcharge. Shared kitchen facilities are available for self-catering purposes.

Laundry service is rendered on-site at an extra charge. On-site parking is available and security guards watch the lodge”s premises.

Gabe Guest House is a 10-minute drive away from Cape Coast-Takoradi Road.
