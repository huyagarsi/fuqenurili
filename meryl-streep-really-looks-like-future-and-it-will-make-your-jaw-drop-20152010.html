Odd yet glorious! Meryl Streep may be buddies with 50 Cent, but there's another rapper that she has more in common with — Future. Fans of the stars are in a tizzy because the two apparently, well, look crazy alike.

Side-by-side photos of the stars went viral on social media last month, yet many are still dumbfounded by the humorous find. Some commented that both have the same bone structure, while others teased that they must be related.

In the snapshots, Streep, 66, and the "Commas" entertainer, 31, both showed off their pearly whites in a similar grin. They lifted their heads ever so slightly (seriously the same, right?!), and kept their long locks down.

"Someone tell Meryl Streep I love her Future costume," one tweeter wrote on Monday, Oct. 19. Earlier this month, a second noted: "Chris leaned over and said, 'I keep thinking about Meryl Streep' halfway into future's set."
