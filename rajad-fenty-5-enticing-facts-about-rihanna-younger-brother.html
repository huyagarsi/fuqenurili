Rajad Fenty: 5 Enticing Facts About Rihannas Younger Brother

Publish date: 2024-06-24

Rajad Fenty grew up alongside his big sister Rihanna who is the best musical artist of this generation. He has appeared in numerous events with his sister and has a close bond with various celebrities.

Rajad Fenty is popularly known as the younger brother of Barbadian singer Rihanna. Born in Saint Michael, Barbados, Rihanna is regarded as the second-best-selling female music artist of all time.

The Fenty siblings share a close bond as evinced by their social media posts. Despite the media exposure, Rajad prefers to stay away from the limelight.

So, know some interesting facts about Rihanna’s brother Rajad Fenty.

1. Rajad Fenty Is Barbadian By Nationality

Fenty was born to Ronald Fenty and Monica Braithwaite on 15 April 1996 in Barbados. His father was a warehouse supervisor and his mother was an accountant Monica.

The 26-year-old was raised by his parents in Bridgetown, the largest city in Barbados. He attended The St. Michael School in his hometown.


Rajad has mixed ancestry from his family side. His father is of African, Irish, English, and Scottish descent whereas his mother is Afro-Guyanese.

As of now, he resides in Holetown, Barbados.

2. Rajad Fenty & Rihanna Are Perfect Siblings

Rajad shares a close bond with his celebrity sister Rihanna as evinced by his social media posts. He has shared numerous pictures with his sister on special occasions.


As a child, he used to visit the sets of her music videos and shows of her elder sister. Moreover, he has met numerous celebrities including English singer Sam Smith, English actress Cara Delevingne and many more.


Fenty even got an opportunity to meet American rapper Jay-Z on his 11th birthday back in 2007.

3. Rajad Has A Brother & Three Half-Siblings Aside Rihanna

Rajad’s parents got divorced in 2002 when he was six years old. He is the youngest of three children and has two biological siblings: Rihanna and a brother named Rorrey Fenty.


Rorrey, aged 33, is also a hip-hop artist who goes by the stage name Gallest. His first mixtape, In My Mind, was released in 2012, followed by a mixtape, Intolerable Cruelty, which was released in 2014.

Moreover, Rajad has three half-siblings: two sisters Samantha Fenty and Kandy Fenty, and a brother named Jamie Fenty. Samantha, Kandy, and Jamie were born from his father’s previous relationships.

4. Rajad Fenty & His Girlfriend Welcomed A Baby In 2020

Rajad is currently dating a musician girlfriend named Aron. Known by the stage name Nora, she is a verified artist on Spotify. Likewise, she released a song named Higher Ground in 2017.


Fenty and his girlfriend are the parents of a baby boy named Reishi Rajad Fenty. They welcomed their newest family member at the Christmas of 2020.

5. Rajad Fenty Is A Social Media Star

Rajad seems to be a social media star and model by profession. He has amassed numerous fan-following on his social media accounts.


As of now, he has over 151k Instagram followers and more than 37k followers on his Twitter. He is also s fashion enthusiast as evinced by his Instagram pictures. Moreover, he attended Paris Fashion Week in 2017.

Fenty now enjoys a net worth in the millions. Meanwhile, his sister Rihanna has a net worth of $1.7 billion as per Forbes.
