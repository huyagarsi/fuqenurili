Lea Michele: I Have 14 Tattoos

Publish date: 2024-08-03

Her Glee character is a total goody-goody, but in real life Lea Michele has quite an edge, at least when it comes to body ink.

"I do have 14 tattoos, but I also do come home every single night and watch reality TV with my cat," the star, 24, revealed in the latest issue of Prestige.

Clad in a curve-hugging, embellished bustier-style ensemble with an edgy black manicure and smoldering makeup, Michele strikes a sultry pose on the magazine's March cover.

In the interview she also talks about smooching her New Year's Eve co-star Ashton Kutcher.

"I come from Broadway. I've done a lot of things. That's child's play compared to some of the stuff that I've done."
