Besides his tremendous ice hockey career, Andrew Cogliano enjoys all the bliss from his personal wife. He is married to his television actress wife for more than three years and also co-parents a beautiful daughter with his fiancé.

Andrew Cogliano is a Canadian professional ice hockey player who currently plays for the NHL side, Dallas Stars. He formerly played for two other NHL teams.

Andrew Cogliano Bio & Family; Age, Mother, Brother, Height

Born as Andrew Cogliano on 14 June 1987, in Toronto, Ontario, Canada, the 33-years-of-age player grew up in Woodbridge, Ontario. He is among the two sons of his mother, Teri Cogliano. Andrew’s brother is named Matthew Cogliano.

For his studies, he joined St. Michael’s College School. Besides, he started out playing minor ice hockey for the City of Vaughan Hockey Association and the Vaughan Rangers in the Ontario Minor Hockey Association (OMHA) before playing in the Greater Toronto Hockey League (GTHL) with the AAA Vaughan Kings. He later enrolled at the University of Michigan and played for the University of Michigan Wolverines.

Standing tall at a height of 5 feet and 10 inches (1.78 meters), Andrew comes with white ethnicity and his birth sign is Gemini.

His NHL Career, Salary & Net Worth

Andrew penned a professional contract with Edmonton Oilers of the NHL in 2005. He went on to play his first game for the Oilers on 4 October 2007, against the San Jose Sharks. Eventually, he was traded to the Anaheim Ducks on 12 July 2011, where he played for 8 seasons.

During the 2017–18 season, on 12 January 2018, the Ducks re-signed Andrew to a three-year, $9.75 million contract extension with the motive to keep him under contract until the end of the 2020–21 season. Later on 14 January 2019, Andrew was traded by the Ducks to the Dallas Stars.

For the 2020-2021 season, Andrew is set to receive a salary of $3,250,000. From his professional career, Andrew bags a mammoth net worth that dwells above $12 million.

Who Is Andrew Cogliano’s Wife? His Married Life, Daughter

Andrew leads a compelling married life with his wife of around three years, Allie Bertman. Allie is a TV actress known for Sucker Punch (2011), Mr. D (2012), and Fringe (2008).

Andrew and Allie shared the wedding vows in 2017. Initially, they began their romance in 2010. Following their nuptial bond, the couple welcomed a beautiful daughter named Lottie Cogliano on 17 February 2019.
