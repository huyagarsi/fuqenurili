Melora Hardin Plastic Surgery Before and After

Publish date: 2024-07-22

Melora Hardin was born on June 29, 1967. Her first acting gig was as a child in an episode of Police Story. She next joined the main cast of the series Thunder as Cindy Prescott. On the big screen, she made her debut in 1979’s The North Avenue Irregulars. From 1980 to 1981, she played Micki Carroll in the show Secrets of Midland Heights. The rest of the ’80s saw her star as Frances “Baby” Kellerman in the series Dirty Dancing. She also performed in the films Iron Eagle and Big Man on Campus.

In the ’90s, she mostly had guest roles on the small screen. She also starred in some movies and TV films. By the 2000s, she landed the part of Barbara Arno in Cover Me. She then gained further recognition as Trudy Monk in Monk. From 2005 to 2013, she played Jan Levinson in the comedy series The Office. Simultaneously, she also had roles in the films The Hot Chick, The Dukes, The Comebacks, 27 Dresses, Hannah Montana: The Movie, and 17 Again. She’s also best known for playing Roxie Rutherford in The Wedding Band and Tammy in Transparent. As of 2017, she plays Jacqueline in the series The Bold Type. Meanwhile, her latest movie was in 2015’s Self/less.

Has Melora Hardin had Plastic Surgery?

She’s turning 51 this year but the film and television star remains youthful. Could this be a case of plastic surgery? Or is the actress’ healthy lifestyle enough to keep her looking fabulous? In an interview, Hardin got candid about her thoughts on plastic surgery. She said:

“Plastic surgery has become a weird Hollywood addiction. The more people buy into it, the more power it has over us. It’s definitely not something I am doing or going to do. I hope to grow old gracefully and make the most of what God gave me.” Simply put, the actress prefers to age gracefully rather than to resort to cosmetic procedures.
