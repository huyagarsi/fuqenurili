Bridget Marquardt Is Engaged to Nick Carpenter: Spider Engagement Ring

Publish date: 2024-08-16

Is that really her ring?! Hugh Hefner's ex and former Girls Next Door star Bridget Marquardt is engaged to her longtime director boyfriend, Nick Carpenter, she announced via Instagram on Thursday, Oct. 1.

"Happy Oct. 1st!!!" Marquardt, 42, wrote, alongside a snap of her hand holding his. "Oh…and… Did I mention @nicholascarpenter and I are engaged?"

While it remains unknown whether the ring on Marquardt's hand is really her engagement bling, the self-proclaimed "Queen of Halloween" received a diamond-encrusted piece of jewelry in the shape of a spider. In the background, she held up a glittered sign that read, "Till Death Do Us Part."

Marquardt and Carpenter first met at the famed Playboy Mansion, where she once lived with such fellow Playmates as Holly Madison and Kendra Wilkinson, and the two started dating back in 2008.
